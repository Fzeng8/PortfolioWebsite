Hi! I’m Faith, a new media artist and designer based in New York City. I am a recent New York University graduate who studied Interactive Media Arts and minored in producing and art history.

IT ME IN PUERTO RICO 2019!

I grew up loving all types of arts and crafts from drawing to clay miniatures to photography to embroidery. During in college, I have become more focused on product design, interaction design, and installations, but I still find myself wanting to try and combine different types of media. Regardless of my medium, I love to share stories through my work and create happy and playful experiences for others.

Outside of my digital work, I sell handmade jewelry and art at local art markets under the name Sad Boiz Co and I am a co-founder of AAPI Creatives & Entrepreneurs (ACE) .

In my down time, you can find me exploring art museums, messing around with yarn, and trying new recipes in my kitchen.
