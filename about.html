I grew up loving all types of arts and crafts from drawing to clay miniatures to photography to embroidery. During high school and now in college, I have become more focused on video and augmented reality, but I still find myself wanting to try and combine different types of media. Regardless of my medium, I love to share stories through my work and create happy and playful experiences for others.

Outside of my digital work, I sell handmade jewelry online (and at local art markets before the pandemic). Sunset Cherry Designs is my polymer clay earrings store and Sad Boiz Co. is an art shop made with my friends.

In my down time, you can find me exploring art museums, messing around with my cameras, and listening to podcasts from DIVE Studios.
